ITINERARY

  • Day 1 Afternoon : SUN : DELHI Depart :
    Check-In starts 4.00 p.m. onwards at Safderjung Railway Station (Near Motibagh Flyover) Suggest Report by 430 p.m. for check-in formalities of Royal rajasthan On Wheels Train Tour After traditional welcome,guests are be escorted to your respective sleeping chambers wagon/saloon. Train departs by 5.30 p.m. & Dinner on board.
  • Day 2: MON : JODHPUR - Fort Town :
    Train arrives by 8.00 a.m.at Mandore railway station At 9 a.m. board a/c buses & visit late massive complex of Mehrangarh Fort Palace ,built atop a hill, in late 16th century Nearby is located late 19th century built royal cenotaphs of Jaswant Thada.Proceed to visit museum at Hotel Umaid Bhawan Palace with rich artefacts and private collection of H.H. Maharaja of Jodhpur Feast on Lunch in 5 Star Hotel & Return to Railway station by 3.30 p.m. Train departs by 4.30 p.m..Time at leisure & Dinner On board.
  • Day 3 Morning : TUE : UDAIPUR - Lake City
    Train arrives by 7 a.m. By 8.30 a.m. board A/C buses & proceed to visit magnificently built City Palace Complex,spread over 7 layers & continuously built over 250 years since it's foundation stone was laid in late 16th century. Also visit nearby located Crystal gallery,with personal collection of Maharaja Of Udaipur. Enjoy Boat ride in Lake Pichola,around the famous Lake Palace Hotel,perched on a small island & After lunch in 5 Star Hotel return to Railway station by 2.15 p.m. & train departs by 2.30 p.m.
  • Day 3 Afternoon : TUE : CHITTORGARH FORT
    Train arrives by 4.15 p.m. at Chittorgarh Railway Station. By 4.45 p.m.board A/c buses & drive uphill through narrow steep road to reach ruins of Chittorgarh Hill fort,spread across a huge plateau.Occupied & ruled by Same Dynasty ,continuously from 10th to 16th century A.D,the bravery of Chittorgarh Rajput Warriors is well recorded in history books..The tragic mass suicides by royal ladies,preferring to jump into giant fire pits (guhars) rather than being at mercy of victorious enemy armies is still an annual day of mourning & pilgrimage at the fort site. In the complex,is also a temple dedicated to Princess Meerabai,who preferred to compose hymns & poems in praise of Lord Krishna After sunset,enjoy Sound & Light Show in Fort premises.Return to train by 8.00 p.m. for Dinner. Train departs by 11 p.m.
  • Day 4 Morning : WED : RANTHAMBHORE - Tiger Reserve
    Train arrives by 0530 a.m.at Sawai Madhopur Railway station. At 6 a.m. board open to air 15 seater canter trailers & proceed for excursion to Ranthambhore Tiger Reserve. Besides Tigers,the other inhabitants of Ranthambhore Jungle include Panthers, Sloth bears, Pythons, Marsh crocodiles, variety of deer & bird species. Return to Sawai Madhopur Railway Station by 9.15 a.m. & train departs by 9.30 a.m. Time free to freshen up & late breakfast
  • Day 4 Afternoon : WED : JAIPUR - Pink City
    Train Arrives in Jaipur by 1130 a.m. By 1145 a.m. board A/C buses & proceed for excursion to 16th Century built Amber fort Palace,atop a small hillock Enjoy elephant joy ride up fort's steep ramparts & time free to discover the Palace Complex. Lunch in Restaurant located in the Fort. Later visit 18th century built City palace,located in the Pink city & nearby located UNESCO World Heritage site of Jantar Mantar (operational sun dial). Return to the train by 6 p.m.for brief rest & to freshen up At 7.30 p.m. proceed by buses to 5 Star Hotel & enjoy cultural program, followed by dinner Again Return to the Railway Station by 1000 p.m. & train departs by 1015 p.m.
  • Day 5: THURS : KHAJURAHO - Kama Sutra Temples
    Train Arrives by 0930 a,m. at Khajuraho Railway Station. The erotic kama sutra Hindu & jain temple clusters,built between 10th to 12th century are an UNESCO World Heritage site. Board A/C buses by 10 a.m. & proceed to visit more popular Western Group of Hindu temples After Lunch in 5 Star Hotel,visit Southern Group of Jain Temples. Depart from Khajuraho railway Station by 8 p.m.
  • Day 6: FRI : VARANASI : The Holy Ganges & Sarnath
    When train arrives in Varanasi at 6 a.m.,drive to ancient Ghats of Holy River Ganges & enjoy boat ride to view traditional bathing rites & rituals of Hindu priests & pilgrims Return to train by 9 a.m. for breakfast. By 10 a.m. board A/C buses & proceed for excursion to UNESCO World Heritage site of Sarnath,where Lord Buddha preached his first sermon in 5th century B.C.. Drive back to Varanasi & after lunch in 5 Star Hotel,return to train for brief rest At 4 p.m. drive back to Ghat area & time free to discover on foot the narrow lanes of this 3000 years town with living heritage. Evening photo opportunity to capture lamp immersion ceremony in Holy ganges river Return to Railway station by 730 p.m. for dinner on board train departs by 9 p.m.
  • Day 7: SAT : AGRA - City of Taj mahal
    Train arrives at Railway Station by 10.00 a.m. At 1015 a.m. board A/C buses & proceed to visit UNESCO World Heritage Site of Agra Fort,seat of power of Mughal Dynasty from 16th to 18th Century. After Lunch at 5 Star Hotel, Afternoon visit UNESCO World Heritage Site of TAJ MAHAL ,also one of seven wonders of modern world Time free for shopping & return to Railway station by 6.30 p.m. Time at leisure & Dinner on Board Train departs by 9 p.m.
  • Day 8: Morning : SUN : DELHI return
    Train reaches Delhi Safderjung Railway Station by 4 a.m. early morning. Check-out by 0730 a.m., after early breakfast. Tour ends train leaves platform by 745 a.m.

ABOUT PLACES ON WHEELS

Palace on Wheels

The Palace on Wheels travel package takes you to a vibrating journey to the royal land of sand dunes and regal palaces. Visit the majestic expanse where Royal Rajputs lived in a grand style. Explore this imperial land on the Palace on Wheels, the luxurious train of India that carries with it an intrinsic ambience that goes perfectly well with the majestic charm and beauty spread so lavishly here and there in Rajasthan. The Palace on wheels is rated as one of the top ten luxury train journeys in the world. It offers you a heart-rending trip to the splendid forts and palaces of Rajasthan in just seven days. Also discover the royal history of Taj Mahal in Agra. The journey to the hinterland of the desert starts every Wednesday evening at 1800 hrs from Delhi and ends on the following Wednesday early in the morning at 0730 hrs.

The Royal Background of the Palace on Wheels

On rolling back wheels of time, we find the rulers of princely states of Rajputana, Gujarat, the Nizam of Hyderabad and the Viceroy of British India travelled by these luxurious coaches. After independence, these coaches were parked and remained in this state for a quarter of a century until in 1981-82, when the Rajasthan Tourism Development Corporation and Indian Railways launched a special heritage tourist train for providing a unique memorable experience to the tourists visiting Rajasthan. The name Palace on Wheels was derived from it's royal background of the coaches. Gradually State govt. and railway department brought many infrastructural changes in the coaches while keeping the same aesthetics of the interiors alive, matching the interiors of the royal state. The train was replaced by the air-conditioned broad gauges new train, which had 14 saloons, 2 restaurant cum kitchen cars, one Bar cum Lounge and 4 service Cars. The cabins of each saloon are attached with bath and shower. The train also has a well-appointed lounge, library and bar.

Its all about Luxury and comfort

That's true! On the Palace on Wheels your journey is full of exuberance and luxurious feeling. A perfect chance to relive the era when kings and queens ruled, when royalty spelt magic. When charm and mysticism was in air. The train is completely self- sufficient and is fully air-conditioned, comprising of 14 deluxe saloons. Especially facilitated for you with all modern amenities of everyday life, you would find the royal past haunting you in the historical coaches. The two dining cars are decked up in true Rajasthani style and each saloon has personal attendants, khidmatgars, for your service. Step aboard for a journey into the past on a luxury train.
